Boston Chinatown Neighborhood Center (BCNC)



Description:

The mission of BCNC is to ensure that the children, youth, and families we serve have the resources and supports they need to achieve greater economic success and social well-being. There are numerous programs such as Pao Arts Center, Red Oak After School & Summer Program, Parent Education, etc.



I have been volunteering as a mentor for the College Access and Post-Secondary (CAPS) Program since 2022. I greatly enjoy getting to be a part of the high school students' post-secondary exploration journey. We meet weekly for college application support, resume workshops, art portfolio creations, and so much more.



One of the youth gifted me one of my most treasured gifts as he headed off to art school - a personal digital drawing of his favorite flower - the gypsophila. It's these moments that really make me feel so happy to be able to participate in and foster such a community
