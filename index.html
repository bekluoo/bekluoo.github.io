ABOUT ME


Hello! Since graduating Emory University Spring of 2022 with a double major in Computer Science and Economics, I have been working at Bain & Company in the Tech Insights Group . It's a role I greatly enjoy at intersection of finance and tech to understand the commercial implications of tech-enabled targets all while working with a tight-knit team of consultants and technologists. I am currently an MBA candidate at the Yale School of Management to further engage my interests at the intersection of tech and business.


In my free time, I am extremely passionate about closing the education gap of underrepresented student populations and love to dabble in all things art related! This website was created as a side project for me to organize my hobby portfolios as well as technical projects. Thank you for stopping by and feel free to reach out!
