Community Learning Center of Cambridge



Description:

The Cambridge Community Learning Center (CLC) provides free classes and programs to adult learners. The staff and volunteers aid students reach their potential through education, skills development, and community participation. CLC offers more than 15 different types of classes, including English classes, and job training programs.



As an ESOL tutor, I'm able to interact with a matched adult learner on a flexible, weekly basis. Aside from tutoring, I love chatting and getting to know the people in the program!



Friends of the CLC is an all-volunteer, 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization that supports adult education at the CLC through fundraisers, scholarships, etc.. The board is filled with extremely passionate individuals and I'm excited to witness the continued partnership.
